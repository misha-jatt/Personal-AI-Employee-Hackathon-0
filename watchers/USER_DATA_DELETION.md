# User Data Deletion Instructions

To request deletion of your data used by the Personal AI Employee app:

1. Email us at: **mishajatt960@gmail.com**
2. Include your account email address and a description of your request.
3. We will process your deletion request within 7 business days.

## What Data We Store

- LinkedIn and Gmail OAuth tokens (stored locally on your machine)
- Automation logs (stored locally in the `/Logs` directory)

## What Happens After Deletion

- All locally stored tokens and credentials associated with your account will be removed.
- Audit logs referencing your data will be purged.

For any questions, contact: **mishajatt960@gmail.com**
